Podcast

This is the official Tanz+Klangkombinat Podcast. Beside our artists it contains other well named DJs and Producers, or even fresh jung talents, that pleased us with their great sound. There will be a new episode every six weeks. If you think we could like your music as well, feel free to apply to make one of the next episodes. Just contact us on our website.
